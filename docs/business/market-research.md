## 2. BNPL Crisis — The Market Gap We're Solving

### Saudi BNPL Landscape

| Provider | Model | Risk | Market Share (est.) |
|----------|-------|------|---------------------|
| **Tabby** | Buy now, pay later. No salary assignment. | Approves based on limited data. Default risk high. | ~45% |
| **Tamara** | Similar BNPL model. | Same risk profile. | ~35% |
| **Others** (Spotii, etc.) | Smaller players. | Same model. | ~20% |

### The Problem No One Is Solving

- BNPL providers approve loans based on salary + basic SIMAH check
- They do NOT know: spending behavior, impulse patterns, actual disposable income
- Result: users accumulate multiple BNPL commitments → end-of-month shock
- SAMA is tightening BNPL regulations (2025-2026)

### SAMA Study — 79% Unconscious Spending

> SAMA Working Paper: "Measuring Financial Capability: The Context of Saudi Arabia"
> Key finding: The majority of Saudis do NOT track their spending. Many exhibit high financial impulsivity. There is a gap between financial knowledge and behavior.

**This is the problem Azdal solves at its core.**
